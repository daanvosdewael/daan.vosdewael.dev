About me

With nearly 10 years of full-stack web development experience, I have primarily build websites for many cultural sectors, including music, architecture, museums and cultural heritage.

My goal is to create beautiful handcrafted sites that represent maintainability, high quality code, great performance and usability.

When navigating the ever-changing world of web development, keeping up with new modern tools and best practices is an essential part of the job. As a self-taught developer, continuous learning has become a fundamental skill for quality work.
